Instructions

Welcome to the Guess the Number Game!

Your goal is to correctly guess the number randomly selected by the computer based on your chosen difficulty level. You will have a limited number of guesses, and after each attempt, you'll receive feedback telling you whether your guess was too high, too low, or correct.

Difficulty Levels
Easy: Guess a number between 1 and 10 (3 guesses)
Medium: Guess a number between 1 and 50 (5 guesses)
Hard: Guess a number between 1 and 100 (7 guesses)


Note: The game will begin automatically once you select a difficulty level.
